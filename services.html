Our Services

Comprehensive care plans for every child’s milestone

Every Pediaveda service blends classical Ayurvedic therapies with allied medical disciplines to deliver measurable outcomes.

Developmental Disorders Growth & Nutrition Behavioral & Emotional Respiratory & Allergy Skin & Immunology Recurrent Illnesses Holistic Programs

Developmental & Neurodiversity Support

Structured assessments and stepwise intervention plans for Autism Spectrum Disorder, ADHD, speech delay, and learning differences.

  • Standardized developmental screening
  • Integrative ABA, sensory, and Ayurveda routines
  • Parent coaching for home generalisation

Outcome Snapshot

Improved social communication, better sleep hygiene, and measurable gains in focus and adaptive behaviour.

Growth & Nutrition

Personalised dietetics to address underweight, fussy eating, gut dysbiosis, and micronutrient deficiencies using child-friendly food plans.

  • Body composition & ayurvedic prakriti analysis
  • Balanced meal plans with seasonal superfoods
  • Therapeutic Rasayana & supplementation

Outcome Snapshot

Healthy weight progression, better immunity, stable appetite, and balanced digestion.

Behavioral & Emotional Wellness

Emotion regulation, anxiety management, and coping skills developed through play therapy, mindfulness, and family counselling.

  • Social stories & visual supports
  • Mindfulness, yoga nidra & pranayama protocols
  • Parent & sibling support circles

Outcome Snapshot

Calmer routines, fewer meltdowns, and confident self-expression in school and home settings.

Respiratory & Allergy Care

Root-cause management of asthma, rhinitis, sinusitis, and seasonal allergies through Ayurvedic detox, immune boosters, and breathwork.

  • Nasya, Dhoomapana & herbal steams
  • Trigger assessment and avoidance routines
  • Lifestyle education for parents

Outcome Snapshot

Reduced flare-ups, improved lung capacity, and resilient immunity against seasonal triggers.

Skin & Immunology

Holistic care for eczema, dermatitis, psoriasis, and low immunity with detoxification, localised therapies, and nutrition upgrades.

  • Blood purification & gut reset protocols
  • Topical applications crafted in-house
  • Microbiome friendly diet guidance

Outcome Snapshot

Soothed skin, faster healing cycles, and stronger resistance to infections.

Recurrent Illness Management

Programs that address recurrent fevers, tonsillitis, digestive upsets, and fatigue by restoring the child’s ojas and vitality.

  • Regular tonic schedules and Rasayana
  • Sleep hygiene & circadian rhythm alignment
  • Tele-health follow-ups to track progress

Outcome Snapshot

Stronger stamina, reduced sick days, and faster recovery from common illnesses.

Holistic Family Programs

Immersive family interventions that combine therapies, parental education, and lifestyle resets for long-term transformation.

  • Weekend intensives and parent bootcamps
  • Integrated Panchakarma & therapy blocks
  • Digital progress dashboards & homework

Outcome Snapshot

Shared understanding, sustainable routines, and empowered caregivers working in sync.
