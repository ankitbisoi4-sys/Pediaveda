Therapy Suite

Ayurveda-led therapies tailored for young minds and bodies

Each therapy track is delivered by specialists trained to blend traditional protocols with modern therapeutic science.

Ayurvedic Treatment Pediatric Panchakarma Physiotherapy Speech Therapy Behavioral Therapy Yoga & Meditation Diet & Lifestyle

Ayurvedic Treatment

Classical Ayurvedic diagnosis (Nidana Panchaka) identifies doshic imbalances, guiding the selection of safe pediatric formulations and daily routines.

  • Individualised herbal combinations and decoctions
  • Dinacharya & ritucharya aligned for children
  • Regular monitoring and dosage titration

Ideal for

Chronic digestive issues, immunity deficits, recurrent illnesses, and metabolic concerns.

Pediatric Panchakarma

Gentle detox and rejuvenation procedures adapted for children to restore balance, vitality, and neurological harmony.

  • Swarna Prashana, Abhyanga, Shirodhara for calming the nervous system
  • Matra basti & nasya protocols for respiratory and neuro concerns
  • Special play-based environment for comfort

Ideal for

Neurodevelopmental disorders, asthma, allergies, recurrent infections, and sleep disturbances.

Physiotherapy & Sensory Integration

Evidence-led physiotherapy, NDT, and sensory integration work to strengthen gross and fine motor skills while improving balance and coordination.

  • Motor milestone facilitation & posture correction
  • Integration of primitive reflexes
  • Parent home programs with video support

Ideal for

Developmental delays, cerebral palsy, low/high muscle tone, and coordination difficulties.

Speech & Communication Therapy

Interactive sessions that target receptive and expressive language, articulation, and social pragmatics in collaboration with parents.

  • Oral placement therapy & PROMPT-inspired articulation
  • AAC integration and visual communication aids
  • Group sessions for social communication practice

Ideal for

Speech delay, apraxia, stammering, social communication needs, and autism spectrum support.

Behavioral Therapy & Counseling

Child psychologists and behavioral analysts co-design behaviour plans that prioritise empathy, motivation, and independence.

  • Functional behaviour assessments & goal setting
  • Play, art, and story therapy modalities
  • Ongoing parent feedback loops

Ideal for

Tantrums, anxiety, attention issues, peer conflicts, and transitions.

Yoga, Meditation & Mindfulness

Age-appropriate yoga flows, breathwork, and guided relaxation to build body awareness, core strength, and emotional balance.

  • Therapeutic asanas customised per condition
  • Breath modulation for focus & calm
  • Meditation and visualization scripts for kids

Ideal for

Improving attention, managing stress, enhancing flexibility, and supporting sensory regulation.

Diet & Lifestyle Coaching

Coaching for families to cultivate balanced routines—covering sleep, screen habits, daily rhythm, and joyful mealtimes.

  • Kitchen workshops & shopping guides
  • Seasonal detox & festive care plans
  • Habit trackers & accountability check-ins

Ideal for

Families seeking sustainable lifestyle shifts for long-term wellness.
