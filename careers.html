Careers at Pediaveda

Build compassionate, evidence-led child wellness with us

We’re assembling a team of clinicians, therapists, and enablers who believe every child deserves integrative, respectful care.

Why Work With Us?

Collaborative Pods

Interdisciplinary squads (physicians, therapists, coaches) co-design plans and share outcomes every week.

Continuous Learning

Access funded certifications, case conferences, and monthly knowledge labs to stay ahead.

Family-centred Culture

We co-create schedules that support caregivers and clinicians with flexible slots and mental health breaks.

Research & Innovation

Be part of studies, pilot programs, and product initiatives that shape the future of pediatric Ayurveda.

Hiring Process

01. Apply

Email your resume, key achievements, and a short note on why integrative pediatrics excites you.

02. Conversations

Meet the leadership team, shadow sessions, and share case discussions or portfolio samples.

03. Trial Week

Optionally experience our pods for a few days to understand culture and workflow fit.

04. Offer & Onboarding

We customise your growth track, mentorship, and benefits to set you up for success.
