Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 September 2024

1. Introduction

Pediaveda (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to safeguarding the privacy of children and families who interact with our clinic, programs, and digital platforms. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the safeguards in place.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact details (parent/guardian name, phone, email, address)
  • Child information relevant to care (age, medical history, development data) with explicit consent
  • Payment and billing details processed via secure third parties
  • Website usage data (cookies, form submissions) for service improvements

3. How We Use Information

Information is used to deliver clinical care, schedule appointments, share progress updates, invite you to programs, process payments, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • Shared with in-house clinicians and allied therapists on a need-to-know basis
  • External specialists only with written parental consent
  • Law enforcement or regulatory requests as required by applicable law

5. Storage & Security

Records are stored in encrypted systems with restricted access. Physical files are kept in secure cabinets within the clinic. We review security protocols periodically.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, corrections, or deletion of personal data (subject to legal/medical record retention guidelines). Contact privacy@pediaveda.in.

7. Cookies & Digital Tracking

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to improve user experience. You can adjust browser settings to decline cookies.

8. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to external resources. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or website notice.
