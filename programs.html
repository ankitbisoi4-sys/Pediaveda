Signature Programs

Structured journeys that nurture body, mind, and values

Pediaveda programs weave therapies, rituals, and parental guidance into immersive experiences with clear milestones.

Why Programs?

Integrated Stack

We sequence Ayurvedic routines, therapies, and coaching so families don’t have to coordinate multiple providers.

Outcome Tracking

Each program includes baseline evaluations, measurable goals, and review checkpoints every 4 weeks.

Community Support

Families access support groups, resource libraries, and live sessions to stay motivated.

Hybrid Delivery

On-campus intensives are blended with tele-consults and home kits for continuity.

Swarna Prashana

Monthly administration of medicated gold (Swarna Bhasma) with ghee and honey to boost immunity, cognition, and resilience.

  • Guided by classical Ayurvedic texts
  • Includes lifestyle counsel and seasonal diet tips
  • Trackers to monitor appetite, sleep, and focus

Duration

12-month cycle with monthly clinic visits and tele-support for interim queries.

Baal Sanskar Immersion

A character-building series that blends storytelling, mindfulness, and value-based living rooted in Indian traditions.

  • Weekly circles on empathy, gratitude, and discipline
  • Movement, music, and theatre for expressive confidence
  • Family participation modules to reinforce learnings

Duration

10-week cohort program culminating in showcase celebrations and personalised feedback.

Seasonal Immunity Camp

Focused on pre-monsoon and winter preparedness with detox, herbal therapies, and breathwork for resilient lungs and gut.

  • Five-day in-clinic protocol with take-home kits
  • Diet reset menus curated per season
  • Yoga & pranayama drills to continue at home

Duration

5 days in clinic + 21-day guided home plan with tele check-ins.

Parent Masterclasses & Labs

Interactive sessions that translate therapeutic goals into doable routines at home and school.

  • Workshops on sensory diets, communication, and behaviour supports
  • Toolkits with visuals, trackers, and habit templates
  • Expert Q&A with therapists and physicians

Format

Monthly masterclasses + quarterly deep-dive labs with small group coaching.
