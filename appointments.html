Appointments

Choose how you’d like to connect with Pediaveda

Tell us about your child and we’ll recommend the right consult—clinic visit, tele-consult, or discovery call.

Consultation Options

Clinic Visit

In-depth evaluation, physical examination, and therapy observations at our Pune centre.

  • 90-minute foundational assessment
  • Dosha profiling and baseline measures
  • Custom roadmap & starter kit

Tele-Consult

Virtual consult for follow-ups, diet reviews, and progress discussions from the comfort of home.

  • 45-minute video session with physicians
  • Digital plan updates and trackers
  • WhatsApp support for clarifications

Discovery Call

Complimentary 20-minute call with our care coordinator to understand needs and suggest next steps.

  • Discuss concerns & goals
  • Learn about therapies and programs
  • Clarify timelines, pricing, logistics
