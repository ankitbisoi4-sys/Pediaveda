Research & Education

Advancing integrative pediatrics through evidence and training

We collaborate with universities, hospitals, and therapists to validate outcomes and share best practices in holistic child care.

Research Pillars

Clinical Outcomes

Quantifying improvements in neurodevelopment, immunity, and quality of life using standardised measures.

Traditional Knowledge

Documenting Ayurvedic pediatric formulations, rituals, and therapies for contemporary reference.

Implementation Science

Studying how multidisciplinary teams operationalise integrative care within family ecosystems.

Current Studies

Ongoing observational and interventional studies are executed under institutional ethics approval with informed parental consent.

  • Ayurvedic Panchakarma efficacy for children with ASD
  • Gut microbiome shifts post Swarna Prashana
  • Parent-led lifestyle interventions for ADHD

Collaborators

AYUSH research councils, pediatric hospitals, occupational therapy schools, and nutrition labs.

Training & Capacity Building

We host CME-accredited workshops, therapist residencies, and digital learning labs to build a skilled integrative workforce.

  • Ayurvedic pediatrics fellowships for doctors
  • Therapy residencies with interdisciplinary mentorship
  • Webinars & certificate courses for parents and educators
