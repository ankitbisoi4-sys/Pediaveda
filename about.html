About Pediaveda

Holistic, science-backed wellness for every child

Pediaveda blends classical Ayurvedic wisdom with modern therapeutic sciences to support every aspect of a child’s growth and development.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be India’s most trusted child wellness collective—integrating mind, body, and emotion—to help children thrive in every environment they experience.

  • Evidence-led Ayurvedic pediatrics
  • Personalized therapy roadmaps
  • Empowered, confident parents

Our Mission

We co-create solutions with families, therapists, and educators so that each child receives the right blend of medical care, developmental support, and lifestyle guidance.

Our care teams stay with families beyond clinic visits—tracking outcomes, refining plans, and celebrating milestones.

Founder & Medical Director

Dr. Vinayak Girish Galatage

Dr. Vinayak founded Pediaveda to close the gap between restorative Ayurvedic care and modern developmental sciences. His approach emphasizes structured diagnostics, therapeutic Panchakarma for pediatrics, and compassionate parental counselling.

He has guided more than 2,000 families through neurodevelopmental challenges, nutrition concerns, and chronic childhood illnesses.

Multidisciplinary Experts

Our core team anchors each child’s journey, working in tandem with visiting specialists and partner institutes.

Developmental Pediatrician

Leads assessments, milestones tracking, and coordinates with allied specialists.

Ayurvedic Physicians

Design condition-specific herbal protocols, Panchakarma, and Rasayana therapies.

Behaviour & Speech Therapists

Coach communication, social interaction, and regulation through structured programs.

Physiotherapy & Sensory Experts

Guide motor planning, tone management, and sensory integration for developing bodies.

Nutrition & Gut Health Coaches

Create meal plans, gut reset protocols, and family-friendly routines.

Parent Coaches & Educators

Support routines at home, schooling partnerships, and sibling inclusion.

How We Work

01. Immersive Intake

We invest time in understanding the child’s ecosystem—medical history, sensory profile, school inputs, and family goals.

02. Integrated Plan

Our physicians and therapists co-create a plan spanning Ayurvedic care, therapies, and home routines.

03. Long-term Partnership

Review cycles, tele-follow-ups, and progress dashboards keep everyone aligned with measurable goals.

04. Community Access

Families gain access to masterclasses, caregiver circles, and expert panels to stay informed.
